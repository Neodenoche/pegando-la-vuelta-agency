The solution

Your hotel,
your channel.

A branded mobile app that puts direct booking, loyalty points, and the full guest experience in your guests' pockets — with zero commission to anyone.

Direct booking engine
Real-time availability, instant confirmation, and a price that's always better than the OTAs — because you're not paying commission.
Loyalty points program
Guests earn points per night, activity, or referral. They redeem for cocktails, surf lessons, or free nights. Repeat stays go up. OTA dependency goes down.
Guest profile & history
Know who's coming back before they arrive. Preferences, past stays, earned points — all yours, not Expedia's.
Push notifications
Last-minute deals, weather alerts, or a personal welcome message when a guest checks in. Direct channel, no algorithm between you.
Activities & upsells
Surf lessons, bar menu, tour packages — all bookable inside the app. New revenue streams without a third party taking a cut.
Owner dashboard
Occupancy, revenue per channel, top guests, and points circulation — at a glance, from your phone.
