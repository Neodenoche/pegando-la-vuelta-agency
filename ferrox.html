FERROX

A dark, high-impact editorial site for a heavy machinery rental company. Built to command authority in a sector where trust and availability mean everything.

Web Design Industrial Dark Editorial
The brief

MOVE THE EARTH.

FERROX needed a site that felt as heavy and reliable as the machines in their fleet. No stock photo softness, no generic construction templates. The design had to communicate 24/7 availability, certified operators, and a fleet ready to deploy — all in under 3 seconds of reading.

The approach

WEIGHT AS DESIGN.

Black backgrounds, compressed condensed type at maximum weight, and a single yellow accent that hits like a warning label. The fleet section uses an asymmetric three-column layout — the hero machine taking double the space — to mirror how a real fleet works: not all units are equal.

HTML5 · CSS3 Barlow Condensed Dark editorial Responsive WhatsApp CTA
The fleet section

THREE MACHINES,
ONE GRID.

Hydraulic excavator, backhoe loader, bulldozer — each with specs, brand tags, and a direct quote CTA. The asymmetric layout puts the most-requested machine center stage without resorting to carousels or tabs.
