The game

Destroy the bears.
Send PNT to the moon.

PNT Defender is a fully custom HTML5 game embedded in a Telegram Mini App. The player controls the Panther mascot, shooting down UFO abductors and bearish candlestick enemies across 13 progressively harder levels — ending with the TR-3B Astra boss battle.

Scores are submitted directly to the Manada Panther Bot and stored in the community leaderboard on Railway.
