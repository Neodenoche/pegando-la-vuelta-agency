Kalmoon

A nocturnal wellness app built for people who come alive at night. Emotional aura tracker, breathing rituals, star map with gyroscope navigation, owl platformer game, and guided visualizations — all in one dark, intimate experience.

The concept

Not a productivity app.
A nocturnal ritual.

Kalmoon was born from a simple observation: night people don't need to sleep better by 10pm — they need tools that match their rhythm. The app guides you through your night with an emotional aura that reflects your mood, breathing rituals, star-gazing, philosophical platformer gameplay, and guided audio visualizations. Built for Android with React Native/Expo, using real astronomical data (Meeus algorithms) and ElevenLabs AI voices.

✦
Emotional Aura
SVG orb that changes color and texture based on your nightly mood sliders — anxiety, creativity, fatigue, sadness. Your aura is yours.
🌙
Star Map · Cielo
Real star catalog with gyroscope navigation. Dual mode: sky view and Space view with real planet positions. Switch to Modo Dios for a 3D Earth with ancient sites and ley lines.
🦉
Owl Platformer
A pixel-art platformer where you play as Kalmoon's owl mascot. World 1 complete (11 levels), rune power-ups, philosophical characters like Alan Watts. Mario Odyssey/Celeste feel.
🫁
Breathing Rituals
4-7-8 technique and others, guided by ElevenLabs AI voices synchronized to a breathing animation. Timer, progress tracking, and a library of nocturnal techniques.
◉
Guided Visualizations
Immersive full-screen sessions like El Campo. Pure darkness, ambient particles, voice guidance, and a minimal UI that gets out of the way.
📓
Nocturnal Journal
14-day history, moon phase tracking, streak counters for nights, auras, and rituals. Your personal nocturnal archive.

The build

Every detail
crafted from scratch.

No templates, no UI kits. The orb is custom SVG. The physics engine in the owl game is hand-built. The star positions are computed with Meeus astronomical algorithms in real time. The breathing animation syncs frame-by-frame with ElevenLabs audio.
