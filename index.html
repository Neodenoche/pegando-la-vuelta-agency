Who we are

Two people.
One relentless agency.

Pegando La Vuelta is Emi + Lorena. We've been traveling South America overland together for over 12 years — Bogotá to Ushuaia, three times. That journey shaped how we work: adaptable, resourceful, always moving forward.

We're a remote-first agency offering the full stack — strategy, design, development, content, and community — without subcontracting anything. One team, end to end.
