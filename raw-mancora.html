The brief

Feel the real
Máncora.

Raw Máncora is a 50/50 venture between Pegando La Vuelta and a local Máncora guide with over a decade of experience on the ground. The brief: build a site that speaks directly to international travelers looking for an authentic experience — not a resort package. Serif editorial typography, full-bleed beach photography, and a tone that feels like a friend's recommendation.

The experiences

Real activities.
Zero filler.

The experiences grid shows 8+ activities — surf lessons, ceviche classes, kite surf intro, beach volleyball, Lobitos day trips — all photographed with a raw, honest aesthetic. Each card is tagged by category and links to a direct WhatsApp booking.

The approach

Editorial meets
coastal.

Cormorant Garamond gives the site an editorial depth unusual for a travel product — it signals a curated experience, not a mass-market tour. The coral accent and teal dark background carry the palette of a Máncora sunset. Social proof comes from two testimonials that read like dispatches from actual travelers, not marketing copy.
