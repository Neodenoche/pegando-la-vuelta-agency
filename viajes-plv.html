The brief

More than a catalog.
A travel identity.

Viajes Pegando La Vuelta is a South America travel agency built by real travelers — not a booking engine, but a curated experience platform. The site needed to convey trust, adventure, and local knowledge while making it easy to browse trips by activity, duration, and destination.

  • Bilingual site (EN/ES) targeting international travelers
  • Trip catalog with filtering by activity and country
  • Community tourism positioning — local guides, ethical travel
  • WhatsApp-first contact flow, no generic forms
  • Social proof with real trip photography and guest reviews
WordPress Bilingual EN/ES Trip catalog Activity filtering WhatsApp integration Community tourism
The solution

Visual energy
that sells the journey.

The design uses full-bleed destination photography, bold handwritten typography for section headers, and a teal brand accent that reads as fresh and energetic without feeling corporate.

The trip catalog was structured to make browsing feel intuitive — filter by activity (Culture, Kitesurfing, Beach Escape...), read a quick description, and reach a human via WhatsApp in two taps.
